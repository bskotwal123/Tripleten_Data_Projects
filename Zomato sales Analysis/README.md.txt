# Sales Analysis for Zomato 

## Overview
This project analyzes Zomato's sales performance, focusing on:
- Sales Trends Over Time
- Restaurant Revenue Breakdown
- City-Wise Revenue Distribution
- Cuisine-Wise Revenue Contributions
- Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

## Objective
The goal of this project is to provide actionable insights that improve sales strategies, enhance customer satisfaction, and optimize Zomato’s overall business performance.

## Key Features
1. **Sales Trends Over Time**:
   - Identified seasonal demand fluctuations.
   - Highlighted months with peak and low sales.

2. **Restaurant Revenue Breakdown**:
   - Analyzed top-performing restaurants.
   - Recommendations for replicating success strategies.

3. **City-Wise and Cuisine-Wise Revenue Analysis**:
   - Insights into high-revenue cities and popular cuisines.
   - Suggestions for tailoring offerings to regional preferences.

4. **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)**:
   - Total Revenue: ₹987M
   - Total Orders: 150K
   - Average Order Value (AOV): ₹6.56K

## Tools Used
- **Power BI**: Interactive dashboards and visualizations.
- **SQL**: Data extraction, transformation, and analysis.
- **Dataset**: Provided by Zomato, including tables for food, menu, orders, restaurants, and users.

## Data Insights
### Sales Trends
- **January Peak**: Sales reached ₹100M due to post-holiday demand.
- **September Dip**: Revenue dropped to ₹55M, likely from reduced promotions.
- **November Surge**: Sales peaked at ₹93M due to increased marketing efforts.

### Restaurant Analysis
- Domino’s Pizza leads in revenue, followed by Kouzina Kafe and Sweet Truth.
- Smaller contributors include KFC and McCafe, which require better promotions.

### City-Wise Insights
- **Top City**: Tirupati (₹43M), followed by Bangalore's Electronic City (₹29M).
- **Opportunities**: Cities like Rohini, Delhi, and Navrangpura, Ahmedabad show potential for growth.

### Cuisine-Wise Insights
- North Indian, Chinese cuisines lead with a 22% revenue share.
- Biryani and South Indian cuisines show growth potential.

## Recommendations
1. **Seasonal Strategy**:
   - Implement promotions during September to address low sales.
   - Maximize revenue during January and November with special deals.

2. **Regional Focus**:
   - Sustain growth in high-revenue cities like Tirupati and Bangalore.
   - Enhance offerings in lower-performing regions like Rohini, Delhi.

3. **Cuisine Strategy**:
   - Promote top cuisines (e.g., North Indian, Chinese) with combo deals.
   - Expand menu options for Biryani and South Indian cuisines.
